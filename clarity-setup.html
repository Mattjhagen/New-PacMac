Step 1: Create Clarity Project

First, you need to create a Microsoft Clarity project:

  2. Sign in with your Microsoft account
  3. Click "Add new project"
  4. Enter project name: "PacMac Marketplace"
  5. Enter website URL: "https://new-pacmac.onrender.com"
  6. Click "Create project"

Step 2: Get Your Project ID

After creating the project:

  1. Go to your project dashboard
  2. Click on "Settings" in the left sidebar
  3. Click on "Setup" tab
  4. Copy your "Project ID" (it looks like: abc123def456)
